Aquacosm data portal

AQUACOSM-plus advances European mesocosm-based aquatic research infrastructures by integrating the leading mesocosm infrastructures into a coherent, interdisciplinary, and interoperable network covering all ecoregions of Europe. This is the AQUACOSM-Plus virtual data portal, providing open access to near-real-time dataflows of mesocosm experiments. To further promote re-use of data, the AQUACOSM-Plus virtual data platform also allows users to access data of past experiments.
